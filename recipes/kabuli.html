Kabuli Plaw Recipe

This is the most famouse food in kabul Afghanistan

I love it a lot, it is really to die for

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • meat
  • Oil
  • carrot
  • damson
  • some onion
  • salt
  • tomato
  • little bit sugar
  • peper
  1. boil the meat to become soft
  2. fry the meat in oil
  3. add the carrot on meat
  4. add damson on it
  5. after 3 to 5 minute add the boild meat water on it
  6. add the rice
  7. close the pan
  8. with low tempreture heat wait to20 minutes
